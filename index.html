✨ How We Met ▼

It all started by pure chance when I was applying to universities. One day, out of nowhere, we crossed paths, and that tiny accidental meeting would turn into something unforgettable. A few moments later, you messaged me asking about how to do something on the application. But here’s the funny part you totally misunderstood and thought I was an elder girl! You actually called me, “Hi akki…” 😂 I couldn’t stop laughing at that.

From there, we slowly became friends… and then best friends. Over the next few years, our friendship grew stronger, even though life pulled us in different directions. I had to come to Kandy for my university, and you were in Colombo. But you said something I’ll never forget: “I know every corner of Kandy. I’ll show you around the city if you want.” That simple line made me feel like I already had a guide, a friend, and someone I could trust.

On that first day together in Kandy, we walked with a huge gap maybe one to three feet between us. It felt a little awkward at first, but it didn’t take long for that distance to shrink. A few weeks later, the gap was almost gone… down to just one to three millimeters. And the funniest part? At that time, you called me “Baba❤️”...not “Machan😂”.
